Bector Restaurant made its foray into the Bentleigh community in February 2008, bringing a new night-time dining experience to the locals of Bentleigh and the surrounding suburbs.

With over 30 years of experience traversing the Yarra Valley, Melbourne, Asia, England and Europe the Bector team is passionate about food and wine and are committed to offering every guest fabulous service and dining experience!

With successful restaurants such as Zartowa and Zest in food to their credit, Bector Restaurant Co-founders Sunil Bector and Ajay Bector set their minds to offering a community focused restaurant and bringing fine food to the suburbs.
